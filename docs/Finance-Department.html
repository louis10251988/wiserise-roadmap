📋 Rules规矩
✅ Verified · 2026-07-21
1. Bot never pays. Human yes for money out.机器人永不付款。出钱要人yes。
2. All store buys → #04-finance new post.店要买→#04-finance新帖。
3. ≥$500 / CapEx → Louis first.≥$500/大件→先Louis。
4. Always notify purchase requester on progress.采购进度一定通知申请人。

6. Cash bag-swap photos → #03-ops, not finance approval.换零钱照片→#03-ops，不财务批。
7. Bank / wire / ACH = Louis + Luna only.银行/转账=只有Louis+Luna。
8. Returned wire/ACH = red same day.转账退回=当天红。
9. 7shifts hours are payroll truth.工资工时认7shifts。
10. Show data gaps. Don’t fake full P&L.要写数据缺口。不编完整P&L。
