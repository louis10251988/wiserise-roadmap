5. Floor — 4 FOH Plays前厅 — 4条话术
Exact scripts. No improvising.固定话术。不要即兴乱说。
✅ 2026-07-22 · Anson Doc2
80/20: Staff never needs Slack. Management runs marketing. Floor only says the 4 plays.员工不需要Slack。管理层跑市场。前厅只说这4句。
Play 1 · Greeting & QR招呼+二维码
When:何时： Right after seating. Job: scan + phone before order.入座后。目标：点菜前扫码+电话。
“Welcome to Gangnam! If this is your first time with us, scan this QR code right now to get 50% off your meal today. It only takes 10 seconds.”
Already used → Play 3. Refuses → OK full price. No data, no follow-up.用过→Play 3。拒绝→原价。没数据就没跟进。
Play 2 · Check drop & review seed放账单+种评价
When:何时： Dropping the check. Job: make them open the SMS.放账单时。目标：让他们打开短信。
“Thank you so much for dining with us! Check your phone in a few minutes — we are sending you a quick link. If you leave us a 5-star review, you get 25% off your next visit.”
SMS fires 90 min from QR scan, not from this line. Line = open-rate booster.短信从扫码起90分钟发，不是从这句话起。这句话是提高打开率。
Play 3 · Loyalty (Visit 2)会员（第2次）
“I have applied your 25% off! Since you came back, let me make you a Gangnam Member right now. It takes 5 seconds, and you get 10% off every single visit forever. What is your phone number?”
Already member → apply 10%. Declines loyalty → still give earned 25%. Never withhold.已是会员→打10%。不入会→25%仍给。永不扣回。
Play 4 · Regular checkout常客结账
“Are you a Gangnam Member? Let me get your phone number to apply your 10% discount.”
Missing phone log can trigger false “we miss you” texts later.漏记电话后面会误发“想你了”短信。
