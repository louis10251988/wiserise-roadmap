🌐 World Brain Overview公司大脑总览
4 layers · 3 identities · 1 loop4层架构 · 3种身份 · 1个循环
✅ Verified已验证 · 2026-07-21 · Louis
Core principle:核心原则： AI manages information, analysis, coordination. Humans own judgment, relationships, responsibility, real-world results. AI管理信息、分析和协调。人负责判断、关系、责任和现实结果。
4-Layer Architecture四层架构
Layer 1 — Foundation第1层 — 基础
🧱 World Model世界模型
The company's complete state: entities, relationships, history, trust levels. Not a database — a living model of reality.公司的完整状态：实体、关系、历史、可信度。不是数据库——是活着的现实模型。
Layer 2 — Thinking第2层 — 思考
⚡ Intelligence Layer智能层
Detects anomalies, judges severity, finds causes, recommends actions. Knows when to act alone vs. ask humans.发现异常、判断严重程度、找原因、推荐行动。知道什么时候自己做、什么时候问人。
Layer 3 — Doing第3层 — 行动
🛠️ Capabilities能力层
Repeatable abilities: hire, onboard, schedule, analyze, control cost. Not departments — skills that cross org lines.可重复的能力：招聘、入职、排班、分析、控成本。不是部门——是跨组织的能力。
Layer 4 — Interface第4层 — 界面
📱 Surfaces界面层
Where humans interact: Slack channels, DMs, forms, alerts, approvals, daily reports, weekly reviews.人互动的地方：Slack频道、私信、表单、提醒、审批、日报、周报。
3 Responsibility Identities3种责任身份
🔧
IC执行者
"Who actually does the work?""谁真正干活？"
Server, cook, recruiter, accountant, AGM, AI Agent. Every action has a clear executor.服务员、厨师、招聘员、会计、AGM、AI Agent。每个动作有明确执行者。
🎯
DRI结果负责人
"If it's not done, who's accountable?""没完成，最后找谁？"
Only ONE per result. Owns outcome, not just task. Has goal, deadline, metric, authority.每个结果只有一个。对结果负责，不只对动作。有目标、截止、指标、权限。
🎓
Player-Coach教练型领导
"When they can't, who teaches them?""不会做时，谁教？"
Knows the work, observes, finds gaps, demonstrates, lets them practice, checks mastery.懂这活、会观察、找缺口、亲自示范、让对方练、检查是否学会。

The 3 identities3种身份
🔧
IC — ExecutorIC — 执行者
"Who actually does the work?""谁真正干活？"
Every action has one clear executor. Can be employee, manager, specialist, software, or AI Agent.每个动作有一个明确执行者。可以是员工、经理、专业人员、软件或AI Agent。
🎯
DRI — Result OwnerDRI — 结果负责人
"If it's not done, who's accountable?""没完成，最后找谁？"
Only ONE per result. Owns outcome not just task. Has goal, deadline, metric, authority. Tracks to the end.每个结果只有一个。对结果负责不只对动作。有目标、截止、指标、权限。跟到底。
🎓
Player-Coach教练型领导
"When they can't, who teaches them?""不会做时，谁教？"
Knows the work. Observes real performance. Finds capability gaps. Demonstrates. Lets them practice. Checks mastery. Builds next leaders.懂这活。观察真实表现。找能力缺口。示范。让对方练。检查是否学会。培养下一层。

DRI:负责人： GM Levi (owns the result, not just the schedule)GM Levi（对结果负责，不只对排班）

Player-Coach:教练： Area Manager finds what GM can't do, teaches GM directly区域经理找GM不会的地方，亲自教GM
Key:关键： Area Manager does NOT just remind the GM. Area Manager finds the gap and teaches the skill.区域经理不只提醒GM。区域经理找缺口并教技能。

5 structural rules5条结构铁律
1. The Brain is the one truth. Every tool feeds it. Nothing gets out of sync.Brain是唯一真相。每个工具都喂它。不会不同步。
2. Hermes touches only Slack + Brain. It never calls external tools directly — tools feed Brain, Brain feeds Hermes.Hermes只碰Slack和Brain。不直接调外部工具——工具喂Brain，Brain喂Hermes。
3. Money, firing, or a broken rule always stops for a human. Everything else, Hermes just does.钱、开除、坏了规矩永远等人批。其他Hermes直接做。
4. Approve by exception, not every time. Hermes handles normal cases; humans see only the weird ones.异常才批，不是每次批。Hermes处理正常的；人只看奇怪的。
5. Check freshness before money or firing. Stale data → stop and ask. Never guess.花钱或开除前先查数据新不新。旧数据→停下问。绝不猜。
